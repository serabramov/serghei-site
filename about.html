About

Finance · AI · Decision Support

I work at the intersection of finance, data and technology. My background includes finance business partnering, financial reporting and controlling in international manufacturing environments.

Over time my focus has increasingly moved toward analytics, automation and the role of artificial intelligence in financial decision support.

I am particularly interested in how modern analytical tools can help finance teams move beyond preparing reports and instead focus on understanding business dynamics and supporting strategic decisions.

My experience includes work in international organizations such as Tesla Gigafactory Berlin and Takeda Germany, where finance functions operate in complex operational environments.
